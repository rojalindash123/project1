Welcome To F Y D .


FYD is a membership website that helps our members find specialist services in different location of the world.So, if you are not able to go the hospital ,you can find a specialist in your door-step.The way it works is, you create an account, select a service you are looking for . The specialists of that nearer location will display in the results page through GPS . You can then click on the specialist details, know more about them and connect to them.If your request is confirmed you will get a confirmation text .We here at FYD want to make this easy for you to find and consult with a doctor of your choice. Once you have tried out their service, come back here and give them a feedback and ratings on how they performed, doesnot matter whether it is good or bad, we want to know. We want to help keep your needs at our priority. As mentioned before, our website is a connecting portal for anyone who is looking for services from doctors anywhere in the world. FYD is being designed especially for your convenience.
